Old fashioned

8/10

Old fashioned is a Bourben based drink. The recipe is:
60 ml / 2 Oz bourben.
15 ml / 0.25 Oz simple syrup(demerara would be the best).
2-3 dashes of Angostura bitters.
garnish with orange peel.

Margarita

7/10

Margarita is a Tequila based drink. The recipe is:
45 ml / 1.5 Oz Tequila(I always do a bit more).
22.5 ml / 0.75 Oz lime.
22.5 ml / 0.75 orange Liqueur(triple Sec).
15 ml / 0.25 simple syrup. garnish with salt rim and lime wheel.

Mojito

9.5/10

Mojito is a Rum based drink. The recipe is:
moudle 6-8 mint leafs(you shold do it with the lime and the simple syrup).
45 ml / 1.5 Oz Rum(I always do a bit more).
22.5 ml / 0.75 Oz lime.
15 ml / 0.25 Oz simple syrup.
top the drink with sprite.
garnish with mint and lime wheel.

Paper plane

9/10

Paper plane is a Bourben based drink.
22.5 ml / 0.75 Oz bourben.
22.5 ml / 0.75 Oz amaro(best would be amaro nonino).
22.5 ml / 0.75 Oz aperol.
22.5 ml / 0.75 Oz lemon.
garnish with twisted lemon peel.
